LIIST.ME

liist.me lets you store playliists of music for safe keeping & reference. Check out a sneek peak of coming features in the top left & enjoy!

.
Enter your email address to get started or see your already saved liists.

These features are in the works.

.
  • user authentication & accounts system
  • private & public liists
  • ability to share link to public, read-only liist for non-authenticated users
  • ability to collaborate on liists with other users
  • commenting system
  • sort & re-order liists
  • Apple Music & Spotify integrations for search & playback

.
